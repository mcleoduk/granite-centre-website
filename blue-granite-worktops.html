Why Blue Granite?

Nature's Most Extraordinary Colour

Blue granite is among the rarest and most visually striking stone available for kitchen worktops. The blue colouring comes from labradorite and other feldspar minerals that reflect light with an iridescent quality — a phenomenon called labradorescence — that changes as you move around the kitchen.

No other worktop material produces this effect. From the deep, concentrated blue of Volga Blue to the sweeping geological drama of Azzurrite, our range of 12 blue granites offers something genuinely unlike anything else. Come and see them in full slab size at our Harlow showroom — photographs simply cannot do them justice.

Lemurrian Blue Granite

Lemurrian Blue is, quite simply, one of the most remarkable natural stones available for kitchen worktops anywhere in the world. Its dark base is swept with broad bands of vivid electric blue, bright teal, and warm gold labradorescence — a mineral optical phenomenon that produces a glowing, living colour effect that changes with every angle of light.

This is not a stone for the faint-hearted — it commands a room. But in the right kitchen, against plain white or dark cabinetry, it becomes a centrepiece that no other material could achieve. Why Blue Granite

The Advantages of Blue Granite Worktops

Genuinely Unique

The labradorescence in blue granite cannot be replicated by any manufactured material. The blue colouring is a natural optical phenomenon — a living quality that changes with light, time of day, and angle of view.

Full Granite Durability

All the practical strengths of natural granite apply — exceptional hardness, heat resistance, scratch resistance, and longevity. Blue granite looks extraordinary but performs just as tough as any other granite in our range.

A Talking Point

Blue granite worktops consistently generate the strongest reactions of any stone in our showroom. Guests always ask about them. They photograph exceptionally well and are the kind of detail that defines a kitchen's character for decades.

Works with Many Styles

The range spans from subtle (Azul Platino, Lennon Blue) to extraordinary (Lemurrian Blue, Volga Dark Blue). Whether your kitchen is minimal and contemporary or warm and traditional, there's a blue granite to suit it.

Heat & Scratch Resistant

Like all natural granite, blue granite withstands high temperatures and daily kitchen use without damage. Hot pans, sharp utensils, and heavy use are no match for the hardness of natural stone.

Premium Property Value

Blue granite worktops are associated with high-specification kitchens. Stones like Lemurrian Blue and Volga Blue add significant visual value that resonates with buyers and adds to the perceived quality of a home.
