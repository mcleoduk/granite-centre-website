# SEO Plan — The Granite Centre

## How Pillar & Cluster Works

One **pillar page** covers a broad topic in depth. Multiple **cluster pages** cover sub-topics and all link back to the pillar. This tells Google you have genuine authority on the subject — not just one page, but a whole network of related, interlinked content.
