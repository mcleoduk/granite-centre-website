Our Strengths

Six Reasons to Choose The Granite Centre

01

Local Essex Expertise

Based in Harlow, we've been supplying and fitting granite across Essex and Hertfordshire for over a decade. We know the local area, and our reputation is built on word of mouth from satisfied customers close to home.

02

Over 200 Slabs in Stock

Our showroom holds one of the largest selections of granite, marble, and quartz in the region. You see and touch the full-size slab before committing — not a small sample or a screen image.

03

Managed Start to Finish

From your first showroom visit to the final polish on installation day, everything is handled in-house. No subcontractors, no handoffs, no excuses — just one team responsible for the entire job.

Precision CNC Cutting

Workmanship Guarantee

How It Works

Our Process

1

Visit Our Showroom

Come and see our full slab selection in person. Our team will guide you through the options and help you find the right stone for your project and budget.

2

Free Home Template

We visit your home to take precise measurements of your space. This ensures your worktop fits perfectly — accounting for every angle, cutout, and edge profile.

3

Cutting & Fabrication

Your chosen stone is cut, edged, and finished in our workshop using CNC technology. Every detail is checked before the slab leaves our facility.

4

Expert Installation

Our experienced fitters install your worktop with care and precision. We leave the space clean, tidy, and ready to use — usually within a single day.
